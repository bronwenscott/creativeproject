Celebrate your friends, family and coworkers with a personalized definition card! Look through the following questions to help you select the perfect word.

  • Does she/he have a title? (Mother, Father, Boss, Best Friend)
  • Does she/he have a prominent characteristic you want to highlight?
  • Are you celebrating for a specific reason? (Birthday, Promotion, Bravery, Courage, Strength, Health)
  • Does she/he have a word they say all. the. time? Put it on a card!
